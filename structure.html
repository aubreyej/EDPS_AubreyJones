Course Structure

This page outlines the general course structure and logistical information for each year. Click on each year to jump to those sections. P1 P2 P3

P1 Course

The P1 CSI course is a 1 hour class held on Wednesdays from 3-4 pm. Students will have pre-work to be completed that will be due every week 30 minutes before class starts. Course activities will include both non-oral and oral activities. They will have in-class facilitation every week. After class students will complete a reflection activity that corresponds to their in-class activity. This will also be due 30 minutes before the next week's class.

P2 Course

The P2 CSI course is a 2-hour class held on Tuesdays from 3-5 pm. Students will have pre-work to be completed that will be due every week 30 minutes before class starts. Course activities will include both non-oral and oral activities. Half of the groups will complete their oral activity first, and the other half will do their non-oral activity first. Facilitators will be available as needed to answer questions, but will not be sitting in the room the entire time. They will always come in during the oral evaluation to grade the students' oral activity for that week. After class students will complete a reflection activity that corresponds to their in-class activity. This will also be due 30 minutes before the next week's class

P3 Course

The P3 CSI course is a 1-hour class held on Thursdays from 12-1 pm. Students will have pre-work to be completed that will be due every week 30 minutes before class starts. Course activities will include both non-oral and oral activities. There will be no facilitation during P3 except during specific activities such as the Escape Room. Content experts and course masters may be available for debrief and follow-up regarding in-class activities. After class students will complete a reflection activity that corresponds to their in-class activity. This will also be due 30 minutes before the next week's class
